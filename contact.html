OUR PEOPLE



AUGUST BRYAN
CHAIRMAN AUGUST BRYAN has served as the Chairman of EMPORIUM since 1993, and until 2007 he also served as the Chief Executive Officer. As Chairman, Mr. AUGUST guides the company’s vision, corporate culture, and innovation strategies. Under his leadership, EMPORIUM has become an internationally recognized technology leader that ranks No. 1 in worldwide sales of motherboards and No.3 in consumer notebooks. For the last several years, Fortune magazine has named EMPORIUM as one of the World’s Most Admired Companies. Since 2013, EMPORIUM has also been recognized as the most valuable international brand from the Philippines for five consecutive years, according to Interbrand.
